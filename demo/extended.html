Layout Utilities

The default body grid centers content at --nc-content-width (720px). These utilities break out of the center column.

.full-bleed — spans the entire viewport width. Useful for hero sections, banners, and images.

.wide — breaks out to 1200px max-width. Good for dashboards and wide content.
